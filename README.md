# HR Core - Sistem Pengurusan Sumber Manusia

HR Core is a comprehensive, modern Human Resources management application designed specifically for Malaysian businesses. It provides a centralized platform to manage all aspects of the employee lifecycle, from recruitment to payroll, while ensuring compliance with local labor laws. The system is enhanced with an AI-powered HR policy assistant to provide quick answers to common HR questions.

## Core Features

-   **Centralized Dashboard:** Get an at-a-glance overview of key HR metrics, pending approvals, company announcements, and quick actions.
-   **Employee Directory:** A complete database of all employees with detailed profiles, contact information, and role details.
-   **Leave Management:** A streamlined process for employees to apply for leave and for managers to approve requests.
-   **Claims Management:** Easy submission and approval workflow for expense claims.
-   **Attendance & Discipline:** Monitor team attendance through a calendar view, log records, and manage disciplinary actions. Includes a QR code system for clocking in.
-   **Performance Management:** Set, track, and manage employee goals and performance reviews.
-   **Training & Development:** A module to manage a catalog of training courses and handle employee applications.
-   **Asset Management:** Keep track of all company assets, such as laptops and phones, and their assignment to employees.
-   **Recruitment Management:** Manage the entire recruitment pipeline from job posting to hiring with a Kanban-style board for candidates.
-   **Team Formation:** A unique visual tool to organize your organization's talent based on roles, modeled after a football team formation.
-   **Payroll Processing:** Run monthly payroll, view detailed breakdowns, and generate statutory files (Bank, EPF, SOCSO/EIS) for submission.
-   **Financial Tools:** A suite of calculators including a detailed Salary Calculator (PCB, EPF, SOCSO), a Retirement Simulator, and an EIS Benefit Calculator.
-   **AI-Powered HR Assistant:** An integrated chatbot that provides instant, accurate information on Malaysian labor laws, statutory contributions, and general HR best practices.

## Modules in Detail

### General
-   **Dashboard**: The landing page for all users, providing a personalized summary of important information.
-   **Employee Directory**: Search and view profiles of all employees in the organization. Managers can add, edit, or remove employee records.

### Management
-   **Recruitment**: Create job postings and manage candidates through various stages (Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, Hired).
-   **Team Formation**: A strategic tool for managers to visualize team structure and roles.
-   **Leave**: Employees can apply for various leave types (Annual, Sick, etc.), and managers can view and approve pending requests from their team.
-   **Claims**: Employees can submit expense claims with attachments, which are then routed to their manager for approval.
-   **Attendance**: Managers can view their team's attendance on a monthly calendar, manually log entries, and generate reports. Also includes a QR code display for easy clock-in.
-   **Performance**: Set individual performance goals and conduct periodic reviews. Both employees and managers can track progress.
-   **Training**: Browse a catalog of available training courses. Employees can apply for courses, and managers approve the requests.
-   **Assets**: An inventory of all company-owned assets. Managers can assign assets to employees and track their status.

### Company
-   **Payroll Processing**: A powerful tool for HR managers to run the monthly payroll cycle, verify calculations, and finalize it. Once finalized, it allows for the download of statutory contribution files.
-   **Financial Tools**: Calculators available to all employees to help with financial planning, understanding their payslip, and simulating future savings.
-   **Pusat Sumber (Resources)**: A page with quick links to official government portals like KWSP, PERKESO, and LHDN.

## Tech Stack

-   **Frontend:** React, TypeScript
-   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
-   **AI Integration:** Google Gemini API
